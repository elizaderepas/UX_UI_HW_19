My
Skills

User Research and Empathy

Understanding the user is my passion and my hobby. I make sure to start every project with questions, not answers. I use empathy to understand people and their needs by being observant and asking good questions. This leads to gaining deeper insights.

User Interviews

I use interviews in my design process to get a deeper understanding of the users' behaviour. I am good at interviewing users and asking the right questions.

UI Design

I strive to design interfaces that are easy and enjoyable to use. I use my user insights and am sure not to lose focus on who I am designing for (not me!). I am proficient in both Adobe XD and Figma and incorporate simple, useful animations to my designs.
